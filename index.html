International Fund for China's Environment

US-China environmental cooperation

Environmental protection is one of the top global issues I care about. It is encouraging to see that China is stepping up its effort in cleaning up its heavily polluted environment. International Fund for China's Environment (IFCE) plays a key role in helping China to ahieve its goal by bringing in existing experiences from the US and the world. It's a great honor for me to work with IFCE on the strategic plan to raise awareness of IFCE’s initiatives in promoting environmental technology cooperation between Chinese and American companies.

The campaign focused on increasing the audience base of IFCE’s key digital communications platforms, from social media, email list, to website visitors. We also conducted outreach with business reporters, managers of American environmental technology firms and Chinese entrepreneurs with environmental investment plans.

  • Service: Strategic communications consulting
  • Date: May 2016

Environmental Defense Fund

Support California's landmark climate legislation

While Trump Administration rollbacks American's climate commitments, California has emerged as a global leader in climate action. I was lucky to be part of the exciting movement and witness the historic moment.

Environmental Defense Fund (EDF) has been helping California design and implement its landmark cap-and-trade program, a market-based measure that sets an absolutely statewide limit on geenhouse gas emissions.

Cap and trade was slated to expire in 2020. 2017 was the key year to determine the future of the program. During the legislation session, EDF initiated a digital campaign targeting districts of key representatives to gain support for cap-and-trade extension. Eventually, California successfully extended cap and trade with rare bi-partisan support and cemented its role as a global leader on climate change.

  • Client: Environmental Defense Fund
  • Date: June 2017
  • Service: Advocacy, Digital Marketing
