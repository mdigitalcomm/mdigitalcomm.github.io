About Me

I'm Miao, a front-end web developer and digital marketing pro based in Charlotte, North Carolina.

My specialty lies in understanding the marketing perspective and developing engaging digital content that targets customized groups of audiences for companies and organizations. My work has driven desired marketing results in combination with digital campaigns on Facebook, Twitter, and YouTube.
